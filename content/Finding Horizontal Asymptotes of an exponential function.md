#math/function/exponential 

- HA’s in exponential functions are easy to find
	- The formula for an [[exponential function]] is $y=ab^x$, the HA is whatever is after $ab^x$, in this case its 0 ($y=ab^x+0$)
	- So the HA is $y=0$ 
	- ? WHY?
		- If the $HA$ is $0$, when we have $y=0$ we have $0=ab^x$, divide both sides by $a$ and we have $0=b^x$
		- No $b$ value to the power of ANYTHING $=0$
