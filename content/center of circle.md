#math/geometry/2d/shape/circle , [[Circle]], [[arc]], [[circumference]], [[radius]], [[diameter]]


- The center of the circle is what it's name suggests, the point where all [[radius\|radii]] come from, where all points on the circle are the same [[Distance]] from
- If circles have the same center they're [[concentric circles]]
- The center of it's circle is its name
	- EG: If we have circle with center $O$ we call it [[Circle]] $O$
