#math/graphing #math/algebra/inequality/equations 

-  Mean data model is a [[Linear Equation]] for a [[scatter plot graph\|scatter plot]] that is a type of [[Line of Best Fit]] → [[Finding Linear Regression\|Linear regrssion]]
	- To calculate the Mean data model, you have to find the [[Mean]] of the $x$ and $y$ [[lists]]
		- The mean of the $x$ list will give you an $x$ [[ordered pair\|coordinate]] and the mean of the $y$ list will give you a $y$ coordinate together making up a [[ordered pair]]
