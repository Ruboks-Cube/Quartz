- Standard form is $Ax+By=C$, where $A$, $B$, and $C$, are [[constant\|constants]] and $x$ and $y$ are [[variable math\|variables]]. If $A$ and $B$ are [[Positive Numbers\|positive]] then it's a [[Inverse Proportion]]
	- NOTE, $A,B,C$ are all [[integer\|integers]]
	- $Ax+By+C=0$ also counts as standard form
