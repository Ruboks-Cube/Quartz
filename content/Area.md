#math/geometry/2d/Perimeter_Area 

- The space IN a 2 dimensional shape. 

- [[2 dimensional shape values]]
	- Area is calculated in $units^2$ meaning it's a [[plane]]. The "units" is for whatever [[unit of measurement]] you're using. 
	- We say squared because if we take a [[Perfect Squares\|Square]] with [[Line Segment\|side length]] 2, it has an area of $4\space units^2$. ([[Multiplication]])
		- The units squared is JUST WHAT IT IS!!!
		- We can fill that whole square with tinier $1$-[[Line Segment\|side length]] squares, and $4$ of those squares will fill up the cube. 
		- ! So we have $4\space units^2$ where the units refer to the UNIT OF MEASUREMENT SQUARED. We have 4 cubes with the UNIT OF MEASUREMENT as the side length and their area is the UNIT OF MEASUREMENT SQUARED
