## Example of a linear inequality
$4x+5>25$$
Again, we ask ourselves, what is x, or in [[Linear Inequalities]] case, what values of x makes 4x+5 greater than 25???

subtracting 5 from both sides aka [[Inverse Operation]] keeps the left side greater, so we are left with $4x>20$$
Dividing by 4 leave us with $x>5$$
Any value of x greater than 5 gives us a value greater than 25 for the original inequality.
