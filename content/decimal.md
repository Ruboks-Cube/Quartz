#math/arithmetic/divisionmultiplication/fraction #math/arithmetic/decimals #math/arithmetic/divisionmultiplication/fraction/percentages 

A decimal in math is a way to represent [[Fraction]]s with a [[Exponents\|Power of 10]], it's a basic [[math]] principal.

we put a "." to seperate decimals from whole numbers.

0.1, is 1, to the [[Negative Number\|Negative]] power of 10. (see [[Negative Exponents]])
0.01, is 1 to the -100, and so on.

These are fractions because 0.1 is 1/10th of 1, and 0.01 is 1/100th of 1, think about money.
