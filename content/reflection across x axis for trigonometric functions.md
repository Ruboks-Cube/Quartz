#math/geometry/2d/shape/Triangle/trigonometry #math/graphing 

- Take the following function:
	$y=-\cos(\theta)$
- How does the $-$ affect the function?
- We know that it flips because all the y values are now reversed, but its not exactly a reflection across the $y$ axis
- It is instead a [[Reflection]] across the $x$ [[axis]]
