#math , [[Euclid]], [[The structure of geometry]]
- The Axiomatic approach has been applied to all the other branches of [[math\|mathematics]], not just [[Geometry]]. 

- An axiomatic system starts with a few accepted statements ([[axiom\|axioms]]) which are then used to prove more statements. 
	- This system has 4 parts:
		- [[Undefined Term\|Undefined Term]]
		- [[axiom\|axioms]] (or postulates)
		- [[Definition\|Definition]]
		- [[Theorem\|Theorems]]
	- Axioms are the basis for proving theorems.
