#math/geometry/2d/shape/circle , [[Circle]], [[Angles]], [[inscribed angle]]

- An angle with a [[vertex]] that lies on the [[circumference]] of a circle whose [[Ray\|rays]] are [[Chord\|chords]]. 
	- The measure of an inscribed angle is half the measure of the [[arc]] it [[intercept\|intercepts]]
	- This is because [[Inscribed angles are half of central angles]] and [[central angle\|central angles]] are where [[arc\|arcs]] come from 

## Problems
- Key points, look out for what [[arc\|arcs]] each inscribed angle [[intercept\|intercepts]]
