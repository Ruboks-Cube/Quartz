#math/geometry , [[Geometry]]

- A line is different from a normal [[Line Segment]]. A line segment ends, a line doesn't. While you can use the word "line" to represent a Line Segment because in essence a line segment is just a line, a line goes on forever.
- The arrows on the ends represent that it goes on forever, this is why a [[Number Line]] has arrows!![[Files/Images/line 2024-05-22 20.52.29.excalidraw\|100]]
- We put lines on [[Cartesian coordinate system\|Coordinate grids]] 
- Lines are 1 dimensional, they are not [[2 dimensional]] ([[plane\|planes]]) or [[3 dimensional]] ([[space]])
