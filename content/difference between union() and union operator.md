[[union()]] and [[union operator]] can both be used to [[Join sets]], just with different [[syntax]]
	The difference is that [[union()]] allows you to join a set with any other kind of [[collections in python\|collection]]/ [[iterable]]
	The [[union operator]] only allows you to join a [[set in python\|set]] with another [[set in python\|set]]
