#math/arithmetic/exponent/radical

The Exponent Rules of [[Exponents]] are these rules to help calculate [[Expression]] and [[equations]] with [[Exponents]] without having to use [[arithmetic]]. 

This helps because, especially with big exponents, they are hard to calculate and very time consuming. These rules will help you save your time when calculating the exponents.
