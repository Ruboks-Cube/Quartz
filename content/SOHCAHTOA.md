#math/geometry/2d/shape/Triangle  [[Trigonometry]]

- SOHCAHTOA is an abbreviation made to help you remember the [[Trigonometry]]. The Abbreviation is meant to come in groups of 3
	- [[sine\|SINE]] OPPOSITE (to) [[Hypotenuse\|HYPOTENUSE]]  (SOH)
	- [[cosine\|COSINE]] [[adjacent\|ADJACENT]] (to) [[Hypotenuse\|HYPOTENUSE]] (CAH)
	- [[Tangent\|TANGENT]] OPPOSITE (to) [[Hypotenuse\|HYPOTENUSE]] (TAH) 
- This means the Sine is the [[Ratio]] (that's what the "to" is for) between the side Opposite and the [[Hypotenuse]]
