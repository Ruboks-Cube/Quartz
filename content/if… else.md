This phrase “if… else” can refer to an [[if statement python\|if statement]] in general

It could also however note that you do not need an [[Elif python\|Elif]] between an [[if statement python\|if]] and an [[Else\|else]]

You can do something like this:

```python
a = 5
b = 9
if a = b:
	print("a and b are the same")
elif a > b:
	print("a is greater than b")
else:
	print("b is greater than a")
```
