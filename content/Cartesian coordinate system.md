# What is it

- The cartesian coordinate system or coordinate grid or cartesian plane is a grid or graph that is used to show [[2 variable linear equations]]
- It has an [[origin]] which is it's center as will as an $x$ and $y$ [[axis]]. The axis are 2 lines and where they meet is called origin. The axis divide the coordinate grid into 4 quadrants.![[Files/Images/Pasted image 20240618101920.png]]
