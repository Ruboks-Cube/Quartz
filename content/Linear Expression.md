# What are they?
- Linear expression: mathematical expression with constants, variables, and coefficients
- Consists of addition, subtraction, and multiplication
- Variables have an exponent of 1
- Written in the form of ax + b (a and b are constants, x is the variable)
- Represents a straight line when graphed
- Coefficient of the variable represents the slope
- Constant term determines the y-intercept
- Fundamental in algebra and used in various real-world applications
- Important for understanding linear equations and functions
