- You have a [[Triangle]], you have to create the [[inscribed and circumscribed circles#Circumscribed Circles\|Circumscribed circle]] of that triangle. Here is how:
	- [[creating perpendicular lines\|construct the]]-[[perpendicular bisectors\|perpendicular bisector]] of 2 different sides of the [[Triangle]]
	- Where they cross is the center of the [[inscribed and circumscribed circles#Circumscribed Circles\|Circumscribed circle]]
	- Place the [[compass]] on the center point, set the width to reach any corner of the [[Triangle]], draw a circle.
