- If we have 3 points of a quadratic, we can get the [[Using Ti-84+ calculator\|Ti-84+]] to graph a quadratic
	- The process is similar to that of a [[Finding Linear Regression\|Linear Regression]]
	- You get the 3 points in the 2 [[lists]], x and y list.
	- Then you select "quadreg" and a [[quadratic equations\|Quadratic]] should be plotted
