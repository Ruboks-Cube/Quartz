A 4 sided shape.  #tag #math/geometry/2d/shape/quadrilateral 

A [[Trapezoid]] is considered a [[parallelogram]], because it fills the requirements of at least having 1 pair of parallel sides.

A square is a parallelogram,  but not every parallelogram is a square because not every parallelogram has 4 equal sides.
