#math/problem 

A friend of mine came up to me and said that 2=1 since $2*0=0$ and $1*0=0$, through the [[Transitive Property]] $2=1$ 

While $2*0=1*0$ is true, $2 \neq 1$ since the transitive property does not apply to multiplying numbers by 0 since *anything* multiplied to 0 is 0.
