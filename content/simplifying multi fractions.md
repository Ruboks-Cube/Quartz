#math/arithmetic/divisionmultiplication/fraction, [[Fraction]]

- Sometimes, in math, we get an [[Expression]] like $\frac{\frac{a}{b}}{\frac{c}{d}}$, or anything along those lines.
- We can simplify these by thinking of a multi-fraction as a [[Division]] problem, so instead of $\frac{\frac{a}{b}}{\frac{c}{d}}$ we instead have $\frac{a}{b} \div \frac{c}{d}$
- We know that [[division is multiplication]], see [[Why is division the same as multiplying by reciprocal]]
- Through this, we have $\frac{a}{b}* \frac{d}{c}$, and our expression is just equal to $\frac{ad}{bc}$
