#math/geometry/2d/shape , [[Line Segment]]

- The length and width of a shape like a [[Perfect Squares\|Square]] or [[Rectangle]] are the sides that are adjacent to each other, or are not opposite. In a rectangle we can consider the top or the bottom the length, and the left or the right width.

- "Length" by itself js refers to the [[Distance]] between 2 [[endpoints]] of a [[Line Segment]]
