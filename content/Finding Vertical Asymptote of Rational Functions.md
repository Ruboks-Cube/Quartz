#math/function/rational 


- To find the vertical [[Asymptote]] of [[Rational Functions]] we have to look at the [[Denominator]] of the function
	- We look at the values of $x$ that will make the denominator 0
	- Those values will be the constant for the vertical asymptote
		- E.G. If $x=3$ makes the denominator 0, $x=3$ is the [[equations\|Equation]] for a vertical asymptote
