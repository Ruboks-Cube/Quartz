#math/geometry #math/geometry/3d , [[3D shape\|shape]], [[Dilation]]

- When all side lengths in a [[3D shape\|shape]] change by the [[Ratio]] the [[Volume]] changes by the [[Ratio]] [[cube\|cubed]]. 
	- For example, if we take a [[prism\|rectangular prism]] with [[Volume]] $V=l*w*h$ and our [[Scale Factor]] is $k=2$ then we have $V=2l*2w*2h \to V=l*w*h*2^3\to V*2^3=2l*2w*2h$.
