So you can use the `|` symbol to combine [[case in match statements\|cases]] or values of the [[expression in match statements]]!!!

```python
day = 4  
match day:  
  case 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5:  
    print("Today is a weekday")  
  case 6 | 7:  
    print("I love weekends!")
``` 
- So for the first case if the [[expression in match statements\|expression]] is 1,2,3,4, or 5 then it will be the first case
