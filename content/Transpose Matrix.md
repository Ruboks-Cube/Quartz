#math/algebra/matrix 


- A transpose matrix is when the rows and columns of the original [[matrix]] change
	- If the original matrix has dimensions 4x5 then the transpose matrix will have dimensions 5x4
	- To make a transpose matrix, you just take the first column, and turn it into the first row, and so on

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1&2&3 \\
4&5&6
\end{bmatrix}
$$
- The transposition of this matrix will be:
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1&4 \\
2&5 \\
3&6
\end{bmatrix}
$$
- If you have a [[matrix#^463438]] like $A$, $A^T$ refers to the transposition of that matrix
