#math/geometry/2d/Perimeter_Area 

![[Files/Images/Area stays consistent 2024-02-15 20.17.49.excalidraw]]
The [[Area]] of a shape stays the same in relation to the other area if you change the length and width by a certain percentage.. No matter what the previous area or length and width were, if the length and width change by some percentage, the area of the resulting changes will be the same.
