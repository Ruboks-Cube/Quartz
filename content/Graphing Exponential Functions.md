#math/algebra #math/function #math/function/exponential 

- Graphing exponential functions is also important to [[graphing logarithmic functions]]
- You first take the part of the function
	- something like $y=ab^{x+c}+d$
	- Parent is $y=b^x$
	- if its a [[Growth function]] then you take values $0$ and 1
	- If its a [[Decay function]] you take values $-1$ and $0$
	- Also make sure to graph the [[Asymptote]] 
		- Asymptote is $y=d$
	- Then you take your values and for $x$ you do the opposite of $c$, so if you’re adding $c$ you subtract it
	- For the $y$ value multiply parent $y$ by $a$ and then add $d$
