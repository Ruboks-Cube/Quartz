#math/arithmetic #tag 
How many times one number can fit in another, OR how many numbers per group.

10/4 is asking how many times 4 can fit into 10, but also "if we split 10 into 4 equal groups, how many per group?" We know that it is 1/4 of 10, 25% of 10, that gives us the answer, which is why division is just multiplication by the reciprocal.
