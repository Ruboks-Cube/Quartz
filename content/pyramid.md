#math/geometry/3d/shape , [[Volume]], [[3D shape]], [[3 dimensional]]

- A pyramid is a [[3 dimensional]] shape. ![[Files/Images/Pasted image 20240719084851.png]]
- The [[Volume]] of a pyramid is $\frac{1}{3}$ times the [[Area]] of the base times the height. 
	- It's the same if it's [[oblique shapes\|oblique]] through [[Cavalieri’s Principle]]. 
- A [[3 dimensional]] object whose base is a [[Polygon]] and faces are [[Triangle\|Triangles]].
