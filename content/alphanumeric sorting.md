Alphanumeric is a method of sorting
	It sorts items based on the typically arranged sequence of letters and numbers
		alphabetically and numerically
e.g:
```python
#alphabetical sorting
mylist = ["banana", "cherry", "apple"]
mylist.sort()
print(mylist) #output ['apple', 'banana', 'cherry'] 
#numerical sorting
mylist = [3, 1, 2]
mylist.sort()
print(mylist) #output [1, 2, 3]
```

There can be ascending and descending alphanumeric sorting
	ascending is the default
		- ascending: A-Z, 0-9
		- descending: Z-A, 9-0
