#Coding/python 


Replaces a [[Strings in Python\|string]] with another string, or a part of a [[Strings in Python\|string]] with another:
```python
a = "hhhhhh"
print(a.replace("h", "H")) # Output: HHHHHH
```
It is important to note that since strings are [[immutable]], the original string remains unchanged
