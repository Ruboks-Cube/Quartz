#math/numbertheory 


A [[Multiple]] of a number times another number will lead to a [[Multiple]] of both numbers.


![[Files/Images/Multiple of a number times another number Rule 2024-01-08 20.49.18.excalidraw]]

AB is [[Divisible]] by both A and B so they are both multiples of A and B. This is an extremely obvious rule.
