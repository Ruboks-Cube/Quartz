- It is useful to know the minimum number of [[unit of measurement\|measurement]] it takes to define a [[Triangle]] and determine its [[congruent\|congruency]] 
	- There are 3 conditions that help logically determine when two or more triangles are [[congruent]]. 


- The three:
	- [[ASA angle side angle]]
	- [[SAS side angle side]]
	- [[SSS side side side]]
	- [[AAS angle angle side or SAA side angle angle]]

- These are true for any type of triangle
	- [[Scalene Triangle]]
	- [[isosceles triangle]]
	- [[Equilateral Triangle]]
