#math/inequality  #math/arithmetic/divisionmultiplication/fraction [[equations\|Equation]], [[Fraction]], [[Inequalities]] 


A technique used to solve [[equations\|Equations]] or [[Inequalities]] that contain [[Fraction\|Fractions]].

Say for example we have- $\frac{x}{y}=\frac{z}{w}$, we can multiply both sides by $y$ and $w$ to get rid of the denominators, $\frac{x*w*y}{y}=\frac{z*y*w}{w}$, the $w$'s on the right side cancel and the $y$'s on the left side cancel, so we are left with $x*w=z*y$. This technique should be intuitive by understanding [[Inverse Operation]].
