#math/geometry 

- congruent means IDENTICAL in shape and size
- If the [[image (transformation)]] in a [[Transformation]] is congruent to the [[preimage]] a [[Rigid Transformations\|Rigid transformation]] has happened.
	- [[Translation\|translations]], [[Rotation\|Rotations]] and [[Reflection\|Reflections]] all keep the shape the same (congruent) since they are [[Rigid Transformations]]. 
	- If you can map 2 shapes on top each other perfectly they are congruent. Image 1 is not                                                                                           ![[Files/Images/Pasted image 20240702102339.png|200]] 
- A formal definition of congruent would be if 2 shapes can map onto each other solely through [[Rigid Transformations]] then they are congruent.
