Conditions are the “check in” point for a [[control structure]] that decide whether they execute the code or not
- They are present in [[selection control structure\|conditional control structure]] ([[if statement python\|if statements]])
	- Also present in [[while loop\|while loops]] 
- The [[Condition in list comprehension]] is different because it’s more about *how* something is done rather than whether it is or not
