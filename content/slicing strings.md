#Coding/python 
[[Strings in Python\|strings]] are a [[Sequence]]
	You can slice strings like [[slicing sequences in python\|slicing other sequences]]
	[[Strings are like arrays\|strings are sequences]]
```python
a = "Hello, world!"
print(a[2:6]) #prints "llo,"(space not included) 
print(a[7:]) #prints "world"
print(a[:5]) #prints "Hello"
```

[[negative indexing]] works for this as well
