#math/arithmetic/divisionmultiplication #math/rule 


In math, anything in [[Division]] thats being divided by itself (the number is both the [[Divisor]] and the [[Dividend]]). The expression will equal 1. Because only 1 of that number can fit in itself.


$$\frac{x}{x}=1$$
