#### Equilateral Triangle Mayhem
The perimeter of [[Equilateral Triangle]] PQR is 24.
Point S is on the opposite side of line QR from point P 
such that [[Perimeter]] of [[Triangle]] QRS is 31. 
What is the perimeter of PQSR?
![[Files/Images/Perimeter and Area 2024-02-12 18.13.13.excalidraw]]
We are given that triangle PQR is equilateral and that it's perimeter is 24, dividing by 3 will give us side lengths of 8. QRS's perimeter is 31, to find the whole perimeter, we add up everything but exclude side QR, the perimeter of PQ and PR is 16, and perimeter of QSR is 31, subtract 8 because side QR is 8 gives us 23, so 23 +16 is 39.
