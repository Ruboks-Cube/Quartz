# How do I find out what number I multiply one number by to get another number?

### Algebraic
Say I wanted to find what I multiply 4 by to get 3. How do I do that? (finding the [[Multiple]] between 2 numbers)

4x = 3
x=3/4

3/4 is what we multiply 4 by to get 3.

ax = b
x=b/a

So why is this? It's because when you divide 3 by 4, the fraction is 3/4, and when you multiply by 4, it's just 3 since the 4's cancel out.

what do we multiply a by to get b? b/a since b/a times a = b because ba/a = b
