- [[Triangle\|Triangles]] are [[similar triangles]] when they have:
	- 2 of the same [[Angles]] -> The 3rd angle will be the same
	- 2 side lengths are [[Proportion\|proportional]] and an angle is the same
	- All 3 sides are [[Proportion\|Proportional]]
