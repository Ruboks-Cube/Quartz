#math/function/rational 


- Bottom Heavy [[Rational Functions]] are those with a smaller [[degree of polynomial\|degree]] over a larger [[degree of polynomial\|degree]]
	- They are “bottom heavy”
	- In these cases, the [[Finding Horizontal Asymptote of Rational Functions\|Horizontal Asymptote]] is $y=0$
