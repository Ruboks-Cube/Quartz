#math/algebra/inequality/equations [[Distributive Property]], [[Negative Number]]


We know that multiplying a negative number by any other [[Real Numbers\|Real Number]] flips the sign.

So if we have $-(a+b)$ where $a$ and $b$ is any positive or negative real number. we have
$-a-b$.
