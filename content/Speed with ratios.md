# Speed Problems

My house is 12 miles from work, how fast do I drive (miles per hour) to get to work in 15 minutes?

House is 12 miles, work is 15 minutes away.

We need to go at the [[Ratio]] of 
12:15, but we need miles per hour so the 15 minutes needs to be 60 minutes.

12:15 = x:60 OR 12/15 = x/60

We get the [[Scale Factor]] by dividing 60 by 15 [[Dividing to find the multiple between 2 numbers]], is 4, so we multiply 12 by 4 since the scale is 4, 12 = 48. 

48 mph.

Here we can also use something called Rate [[Multiplication\|Multiplied]] by Time = Distance.

[[RT=D Rate times Time equals Distance]]
