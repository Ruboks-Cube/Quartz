We can use [[Fraction]]s to represent [[Ratio]]s, and I'm not just talking about [[Parts to a Whole]]


Fractions and Ratios are similar since they both have this property of being [[equivalent]]/[[Proportion]]al, so we use fractions in [[Equation]]s to represent ratios when we need them in equations.
