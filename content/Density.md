#science #math #science/physics 

- The density of an [[3D shape]] refers to how much [[Mass]] there is per unit of [[Volume]]. To get the density you do $\frac{mass}{volume}$.



- Density refers to *how tightly packed* the [[atom\|atoms]] of [[Molecule\|molecules]] are
	- High density means molecules are very close
	- Low means they’re spread out
	- [[Water]] is more dense as liquid than solid (ice)
