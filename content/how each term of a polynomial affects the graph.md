#math/algebra/inequality/equations/polynomial 


- Each term in a [[polynomials\|polynomial]] has a certain *weight*
- This weight gets bigger the higher the [[degree of polynomial\|degree]] of the [[term]] is
	- However, if a certain [[Coefficient]] is big enough you can see it’s affect
		- the coefficient can effectively change the weight of the term
- E.G. in a cubic function $x^3+400x^2+x$ will look like a really steep quadratic for a long period of time
- This is until the $x^3$ eventually gets bigger than the $400x^2$
