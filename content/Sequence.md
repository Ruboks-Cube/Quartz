	- $3,5,7\dots$
	- $21,16,11,6\dots$
	- $1,2,4,8\dots$ 
- Ordered lists of numbers like these are called **sequences**, each number in a sequence is called a [[term]] 
	- Sequences usually have patterns that allow us to predict the next term
