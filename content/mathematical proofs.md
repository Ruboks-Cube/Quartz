#math [[math]], [[Pythagorean Theorem]]
Proofs are extremely important for mathematics because they determine what information you can use for problems. If something isn't proved you can't really be sure if your answer is correct. Imagine if the pythagorean theorem wasn't actually proved, nobody would be sure what the other side length is, or if the triangle is even possible. Proofs also go deeper into the understanding we have in math, they are the "why" that explains how something works.
