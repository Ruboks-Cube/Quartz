Used when something has an exponent that equals

The behavior of this is unexpected for [[Exponents]], and is part of the [[Exponent Rules!]]

It states that Anything to the power of zero, equals 1.

# Explaining The Rule
This rule says that anything to the power of 0, equals 1. 0 as an exponent can appear when you use the [[Same Base rule]] or others
