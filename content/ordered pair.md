#math/algebra #math/graphing 

Ordered pairs represent possible solutions for a [[2 variable linear equations\|2 variable linear equation]].
They can also be graphed in the [[Cartesian coordinate system]]

[[ordered pair\|Ordered Pairs]] come alphabetically, so the variable the first number represents would be the one that comes first in the alphabet $(x,y)$, this is why $x$ comes first.
