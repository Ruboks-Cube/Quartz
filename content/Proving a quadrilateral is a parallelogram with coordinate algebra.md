- The [[vertex\|vertices]] of [[quadrilateral]] $ABCD$ are located at $A(-1,2)$, $B(3,3)$, $C(8,2)$, and $D(4,1)$. Determine whether or not quadrilateral $ABCD$ is a [[parallelogram]] or not with a [[2 column proofs\|2 column proof]]
	- Vertices of quadrilateral $ABCD$ are $A(-1,2)$, $B(3,3)$, $C(8,2)$, and $D(4,1)$. -> Given
	- Find the [[slope]] of each [[Line Segment\|segment]] -> [[Calculating Slope]]
		- [[slope]] of $\overline {AB}=\frac{{3-2}}{3-(-1)}=\frac{1}{4}$  
		- slope of $\overline {BC}=\frac{{2-3}}{8-3}=-\frac{1}{5}$ 
		- slope of $\overline {CD}=\frac{{1-2}}{4-8}=\frac{-1}{-4}=\frac{1}{4}$ 
		- slope of $\overline {DA}= \frac{{1-2}}{4-(-1)}=-\frac{1}{5}$ 
	- $\overline {AB} \parallel \overline {CD}$, and $\overline {BC} \parallel \overline {DA}$ -> If 2 [[Line Segment\|Line Segments]] have equal slopes then they are [[parallel]]
	- [[quadrilateral]] $ABCD$ is a [[parallelogram]] -> Definition of parallelogram.
