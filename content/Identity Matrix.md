#math/algebra/matrix 


- What you multiply by a matrix to get the original matrix
	- Equivalent of multiplying by 1
- The identity of 2x2 matrices is just $\begin{bmatrix}1&0\\0&1\end{bmatrix}$
- Identity of 3x3 matrices is $\begin{bmatrix}1&0&0 \\ 0&1&0 \\ 0&0&1\end{bmatrix}$
- The order you multiply the identity matrix in doesn’t matter, this one of the few cases where [[matrix multiplication#^4ebc52]] isn’t true
