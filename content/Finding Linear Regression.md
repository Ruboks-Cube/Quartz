- A linear regression is a type of a [[Line of Best Fit]]
- You can find the line of best fit with:
	- `STA, ->, 4, Store RegEQ, VARS, ->, ENTER, ENTER`
	- Then select Calculate
	- Using `ZOOM 9` will GRAPH THE LINE OMGOMGOMG

	- to understand, click y= after doing the steps, you will see the equation of [[Line of Best Fit]] in there
