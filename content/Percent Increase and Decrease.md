# What is it?
Percent Increase and Decrease is when you want to increase/decrease a value by a certain percentage. 

Take this problem:

Last year at a competition we had 180 students join, this year we expect a 20% increase. How many students are coming this year?

But we can just think of 180 as 100%, and make it so the other number is 100%, +20%, we are adding 20% to the original percentage.

180 times 120% = 216

- (a) we need $24$ to increase by $\frac{75}{100}$, to do this, we can try to find $75$% of 24 then add that on top of 24, or we can just find $175$% of 24
