#math/algebra/inequality/equations #math/algebra #math/inequality 

What happens when we [[Distributive Property\|distribute]] or [[Factoring\|factor]] [[Negative Number\|Negative Numbers]]?
The sign flips!

We have to watch out especially for $-1$, since sometimes it can be hidden.
Take this [[Expression]], $-(x+b)$, this expression is the result of factoring a negative one out of $-x-b$, but 1's don't always show up.
