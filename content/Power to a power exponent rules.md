# what is a power to a power?
A power to a power is when you have something that has an [[Exponents\|Exponent]] in parenthesis, and on that parenthesis there is also an exponent
![[Files/Images/Power to a power exponent rules 2024-01-25 20.34.35.excalidraw]]
We multiply $x^y$, z times, since thats the definition of an exponent, then using the [[Product of Powers, quotient of powers]] rule, we have $x^y$ times $x^y$ times $x^y$, z times, so we add y to itself z times, which is y times z.


> [!NOTE] The exponents can be reversed
> $(x^y)^z=x^{y*z}=x^{z*y}$
