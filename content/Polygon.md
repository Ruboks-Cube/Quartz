#math/geometry/2d/shape #math/geometry [[Geometry]], [[2D shape\|shape]]

- A polygon is  [[2 dimensional]] composed of straight-[[Line Segment]] that meet at their [[point\|endpoint]]. The line segments of polygons are called [[Line Segment\|sides]], and each endpoint is called a [[vertex]]. Polygons have at least three sides and three angles, and their sides must be straight.


> [!warning] WARNING
> [[Circle\|Circles]] do not count as [[Polygon\|Polygons]] because they are curved.

- Polygons that  all equal sides and [[Angles]] are called [[Regular polygon\|Regular polygons]]
