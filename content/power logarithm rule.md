#math/arithmetic/logarithm 

- The [[power logarithm rule]] is $\log_{b}a^c = c\log_{b}a$
- PROOF
	- $\log_{b}a^c=\log_{b}a*\dots \text{C amount}*a$
	- The [[product logarithm rule]] is $\log_{b}a*c=\log_{b}a+\log_{b}c$
	- Since we’re multiplying many $a$’s together we have $\log_{b}a+\dots+\log_{b}a \space \text{    c times}$ 
	- We can [[Factoring\|factor]] $\log_{b}a$ C times from the above [[Expression]] so we have $c(\log_{b}a)$
