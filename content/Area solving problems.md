#math/geometry/2d/Perimeter_Area 

### Problem 1

![[Files/Images/Area solving problems 2024-02-18 13.30.44.excalidraw]]
I painted the green triangle on this wall with 6 pints of paint. I want to paint the rest of the wall with red. The base of the triangle is 2/3 the length of the wall, how many pints of red paint do I need? 

**We need 12 pints of red paint! Here is why**

1. We first need to set a [[variable math\|Variable]], make sure to [[Files/Images/set smaller values to variables]]
2. We find that the green region is just x times y
	1. Find the area of the smaller region by noticing that it's x times y divided by 2, so it would be 3
3. Find area of other large region by noticing green region and the small region make up half of the full rectangle, the bottom makes up 3x times y divided by 2, so the top should make up 3x times y divided by 2 as well
4. 9 + 3 is 12
