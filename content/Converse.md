#math 

- Converse in [[math]] and logic is when you swap the 2 statements
	- It's like the [[Symmetric Property of equality]] where $1+2=3$ and $3=1+2$
	- An example with words would be "If Cliff is thirsty she drinks water" -> The converse would be "If Cliff drinks water she is thirsty."
- The [[hypothesis]] and the conclusion are swapped.
