#math/geometry/transformation #tag 

# What is a transformation
- A transformation is an operation that alters the position, shape, size, or orientation of a [[Geometry\|geometric]] [[2D shape\|Shape]]. 
- The most important transformations are:
	- [[Translation]]
	- [[Reflection]]
	- [[Rotation]]
	- [[Dilation]]
	- [[Stretches]]
- In any transformation, the original figure is called a [[preimage]]
	- The figure produced after the transformation is called an [[image (transformation)]] 
	- In this image, the preimage is $\triangle ABC$, the [[image (transformation)]] is $\triangle A'B'C'$![[Files/Images/Pasted image 20240627083355.png]]
