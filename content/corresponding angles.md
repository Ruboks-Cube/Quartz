- Corresponding angles are part of the [[corresponding parts]] when you do a [[Transformation]], when there is a [[Angles and parallel lines\|transversal line]], or when 2 things are just [[congruent]] or [[similar]]
- When something is corresponding it means they are related, or is in the same position relative to the other thing.
