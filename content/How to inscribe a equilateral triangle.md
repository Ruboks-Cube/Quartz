#math/geometry/2d/shape/Triangle #math/geometry/creating , [[Equilateral Triangle]]

- We can [[inscribing shapes in circles\|inscribe]] a [[Equilateral Triangle]] inside a [[Circle]], just like a [[How to inscribe a square\|square]]
# How to
- The first step is to find the center of the circle. Then draw an [[arc]] with your [[compass]] (in the picture it's just another circle) and make sure that arc [[Intersect\|intersects]] the [[circumference]] of the original circle. From that, draw 3 points 1 from the [[diameter]] and 2 from the ones where the circles intersect. Connect those points and you have a [[Equilateral Triangle]]
