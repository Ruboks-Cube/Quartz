#math #math/geometry/2d/shape , [[2 dimensional]], [[3D shape]], [[3 dimensional]]

- We can form [[3D shape\|3 dimensional shapes]] by [[Rotation\|Rotating]]--[[2D shape\|2D shapes]]
	- It's like we keep the TRAIL of the [[2D shape]] to create the [[3D shape]]. 
- If we rotate the [[2D shape]] on any axis not [[Perpendicular]] to the [[plane]] the shape stays on a [[3D shape]] will be created.
	- If a shape is rotated in such a way the region of space swept out by the [[Rotation]] is a [[3 dimensional]] object called a [[solid of revolution]]
	- The [[line of rotation]] is sometimes called an [[line of rotation\|axis of rotation]].
