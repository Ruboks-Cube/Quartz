here, we know that a+b is greater than c+d. SO, when we multiply it by a [[Negative Number]], we know that all of the signs flip right? a becomes -a, c becomes -c.

So, when we pay attention to the [[Absolute Value]], we know that C+D is closer to 0 than a+b. So now suddenly, when a and b become negative, -a-b is farther to the left of 0 than -c-d since c+d was originally closer to 0, and we know that being closer to 0 when you is talking about [[Negative Number]] is being RIGHT right? AND WE KNOW THAT BEING RIGHT IN NEGATIVE NUMBERS MEANS YOU ARE BIGGER!!!!!

so there we have it, we flip the sign of the inequality when multiplying or dividing by negative numbers.
