#math/geometry #math/geometry/2d , [[2D shape]], [[2 dimensional]]

- [[composite]], [[Prime Numbers\|Prime]]. 


- Composite [[2 dimensional]] figures are made of simple [[2 dimensional]] figures such as [[Triangle\|Triangles]] and [[quadrilateral\|Quadrilaterals]]. 
	- To find the [[Area]] of such a shape, break the composite shape into simpler shapes. 
		- Find the [[dimensions of a shape\|dimensions of the simple shapes]]
		- Calculate the [[Area]] of each simple shape
		- Add the areas of all the simple shapes
