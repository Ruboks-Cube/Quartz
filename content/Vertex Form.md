#math/algebra/inequality/equations/polynomial 

- Vertex form is as follows
	- $a(x-h)^2+k=y$  
	- "a" is the same as the [[Standard Form quadratic]]
- Vertex form gives us $(h,k)$ this is because when we find the value of $x$ that cancels out with $h$ there is no squaring so there’s no possible 2 solutions for that value of $x$
