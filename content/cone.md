#math/geometry/3d/shape [[3D shape\|shape]]

- The cone represents a [[Triangle]] like shape, where if you Rotated a [[Triangle]] a full 360 degrees on the $z$ axis and you kept the path the triangle took, that's the shape you would get
	 ![[Files/Images/Pasted image 20240718204303.png|200]]
	 - The volume of a [[cone]] is $\frac{1}{3}$ the volume of a [[cylinder]] so $\frac{1}{3} \pi r^2h$
	 - The volume stays the same even if it's [[oblique shapes\|oblique]] through [[Cavalieri’s Principle]].
