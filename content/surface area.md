#math/geometry #math/geometry/2d #math/geometry/3d [[2D shape]], [[3D shape]], [[3 dimensional]], [[2 dimensional]]

- The surface area of a [[3D shape]] is the space outside. Similar to the [[Perimeter]] of a [[2D shape]].
	- We can find the [[surface area]] by adding up all the [[Area\|Area's]] of each [[Face]]. 
	- Unfolding a [[3D shape]] we get a [[Net (geometry)]]
	- Finding the total [[Area]] of this Net by finding the area of [[composite 2 dimensional figures]] we can find the total surface area.
