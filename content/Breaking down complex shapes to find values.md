#problemsolving #math/geometry 

When we have shape, lets say we need to find a [[2 dimensional shape values\|2d shape value]], and the shape is pretty complex. We can usually split the shape up into different shapes, or find the area of something bigger then subtract shapes from it. We are not confined to finding the [[Perimeter]] or [[Area]] of the shape as a whole.

Think about the [[Triangle]] area, we found the area of the triangle by ADDING on another triangle and turning it into a [[Rectangle]], then we divided it by 2.
