# What does perpendicular mean
- When civil engineers lay out plans for new streets, they want to make the streets safe. Engineers want to ensure that when the streets [[Intersect]], drivers get equal sight lines, regardless of which direction they come from. To do this, engineers often design the two [[Intersect\|Intersecing]] streets so that they form [[Right Angle\|90 degree angles]] with one another.
- Whenever 2 [[line\|lines]] or 2 [[Line Segment\|Line segments]] [[Intersect]] at a [[Right Angle]] they are perpendicular. 
- When 2 lines are perpendicular, $4$ [[Right Angle\|Right angles]] form.
