#math/numbertheory #math/arithmetic/divisionmultiplication 

Factors are [[integer]], meaning that by dividing a integer by a number that does not lead to a remainder will get you a factor. Factors are the opposite of [[Multiple]]s meaning that 16 is a multiple of 4, but 4 is a factor of 16. You multiply 4 by 4 to get 16, so 4 is a factor of 4. Factors are the pairs and they are found through [[Factoring]]
