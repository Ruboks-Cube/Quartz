#math/geometry/2d/shape/Triangle 

Find $\overline {QR}$
![[Files/Images/Finding Length of leg with a and c 2024-02-20 19.58.57.excalidraw]]
In this problem we have a [[Right Triangle]] and we need to solve for $\overline {QR}$

We should start by plugging in the corresponding values for the [[Pythagorean Theorem]]
$$
3^2+b^2=(4\sqrt{ 2 })^2
$$
We are solving for b, lets start by simplifying the [[Perfect Squares\|Squares]]
$$9+b^2=32$$This is equal to
$$
b^2=23
$$
isolate $b$ with one last step, square rooting
$$
b=\sqrt{ 23 }
$$
