- When you calculate [[Area]] you do $value \space units^2$
	- For *volume* you do $value \space units^3$ because now the volume of the [[Perfect Squares\|Square]] in [[3 dimensional\|3d]] is a [[cube]] with side length of $1 \space unit$ so its volume is $1*1*1$ aka $units^3$. 
	- [[plane]] figures have [[2 dimensional\|2 dimensions]] while [[space\|solid figures]] have [[3 dimensional\|3 dimensions]]
