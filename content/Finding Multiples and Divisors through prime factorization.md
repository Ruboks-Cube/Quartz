# How can we use [[Prime factorization]] to help us find multiples and divisors of a number?


![[Files/Images/Finding Multiples and Divisors through prime factorization 2024-01-08 20.47.25.excalidraw]]

so, how can the prime factorization of a number help with finding [[Multiple]]s and [[Divisor]]s of a number.


16 is 2^4. This also means that any multiple of 16 has to have at least 4 2's. 

Any [[Divisor]] of 16 must have less than or equal to 4 2's, and only 2's since 3 is not a [[Factor]] of 16.
