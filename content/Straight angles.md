## Whats a linear pair
- A linear pair is a pair of , [[adjacent]] supplementary [[Angles]].
## Straight/Supplementary Angles

If 360 degrees is going all the way around, and it makes a straight line or 0 degree angle since in the 2d space it would just look like a single line, 180 degrees is also a straight line. [[180 degree angle]].

![[Files/Images/Straight and vertical angles 2024-02-09 18.24.26.excalidraw]]
If we have an angle that splits the 180 degrees like this:
![[Files/Images/Straight and vertical angles 2024-02-09 18.25.52.excalidraw]]
The sum is always 180, which makes sense since they are "on" a 180 degree angle. 
The 2 angles are around a 180 degree angle, like [[Angles around a point]]


When 2 angles have the sum of 180 degrees, we call them supplementary angles.
