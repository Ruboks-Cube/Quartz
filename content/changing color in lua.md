#coding/robloxstudiolua 


## changing through scripts

```Lua
local part = game.Workspace.Part --or script.parent

part.Color = Color3.new(0.290196, 0.572549, 1)
```
We change the color of a part through something called [[Color3]], the [[child]] Color is changed through Color3, similar to how the child [[size game development]] changes through [[Vector 3]]. 
## Changing through [[game engine]]
Very similar to [[Changing Size of a part roblox studio lua]], or any other property of a part, you just navigate to properties, find color, and select the color you want.
